Problem Statement: SDG GOAL 3

Imagine the world in 2030, fully inclusive of persons with disabilities. According to WHO, over a billion people (about 15% of the world's population) have some form of disability and People with disability have less access to health care services and therefore experience unmet health care needs. According to National population commission about 19 million people live with disabilities in Nigeria and have little or no access to healtcare

HealthLine: a solution to this problem

HealthLine is a web-app that gives disabled people access to health services by connecting them with health facilities and professional where they can get health advice and diagnosis from the comfort of their homes with their devices.

Vision and Mission Statements

Vision: To enable easy access to healthcare better for the disabled

Mision: Make healthcare better for the disabled, so they can focus on what they do best.

Objective: Create a centralized system where disabled people and healthcare workers,practitioners can easily connect.
